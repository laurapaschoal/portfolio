Pandria
Overview: Creation of prototype product, landing page and pitch.
The Process: Design System, Wireframing, Prototyping.
Amsterdam, 2024
Pandria is a startup that revolutionizes performance management by acting as a personal feedback coach, automating feedback requests, and providing personalized coaching through work chat apps like Slack or Teams. It tailors advice to individual contexts and synthesizes feedback into growth reports, removing recency bias. This enables continuous growth and effective performance reviews.
Wireframe and Prototyping

This project consisted of 4 phases.

The first one was a showcase of a prototype about how the chatbot talks and asks things in a one on one in Slack and Teams, how it integrates with Google Calendar and Outlook, so the CEO of Pandria could show it in action for the investors in a presentation.
Dashboard

The next one was to build a comprehensive design system for the dashboard, which is a side of the product, to enhance user experience and ensure consistency. This system streamlines workflows, making the design process more efficient for both myself and the developers. There are 3 types of dashboards, one for each employee of the company, such as HR people, managers and teammates, where they can share feedback, team growth, key actions to take about the feedback, graphs to show feedback engagement, self reflection and so on.
Pitch

The third phase included a compelling design for the investor pitch, focusing on clarity and visual impact to effectively communicate the product's value. The design aligns with the brand identity, ensuring a professional and engaging presentation.
Landing Page

The final phase involved creating a research-driven responsive landing page, competitor analysis, and using comparison charts. Through multiple iterations, I refined the design to achieve the best result.
